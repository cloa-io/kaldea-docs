---
title: "Slack Integration"
sidebarTitle: "Notifications"
---

1. Go to `Settings` through profile on top right side of navigation bar.

![](/images/notifications-1.png)

2. Select `Notifications` section and turn on `Slack notifications`.

![](/images/notifications-2.png)

3. You will be redirected to slack url and type your workspace url.

![](/images/notifications-3.png)

4. Finally, allow Kaldea App then you will be redirected to kaldea.

![](/images/notifications-4.png)

5. You can confirm Slack notifications status by toggle button is on.

![](/images/notifications-5.png)
