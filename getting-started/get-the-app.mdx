## Overview

Kaldea is available as a mac desktop application and from the browser. For mac users, we recommend using our desktop clients for the best experience.

## Desktop Clients

Kaldea is available for macOS Intel, macOS Apple Silicon. Download the desktop app using the following link:

  <Tab title="macOS Intel">
    [Download link](https://kaldea-desktop.kaldea.com/latest/Kaldea-mac-x64.dmg)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="macOS Apple Silicon">

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

These are the benefits of using the desktop app:

- Independent, clean, and immersive environment built just for Kaldea. No more toggling between tabs.
- Native app experiences such as navigating back and forward app paths.
- Keyboard shortcuts have less risk of conflicting with browser-based presets or custom shortcuts.

## Browsers

Go to [app.kaldea.com](app.kaldea.com) to sign in. We'll launch Kaldea directly in your browser window.
